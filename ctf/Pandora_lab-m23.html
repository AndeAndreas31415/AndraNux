For all the people who use qemu:

    tar -xfv pandora.ova

    qemu-img convert -O qcow2 pandora-2020-04-08-systemd-OVA-2.0-disk001.vmdk pandora-1.qcow2
    qemu-img convert -O qcow2 pandora-2020-04-08-systemd-OVA-2.0-disk002.vmdk pandora-2.qcow2

    mv pandora-1.qcow2 pandora-sphinx.qcow2
    mv pandora-2.qcow2 pandora-admin.qcow2

3.Troubleshooting
If your device are not correct booting, than can you download the stock firmware from Samsung and install it .
The Officle way is create an Account on Mobile Samsung and download the firmware.
The better and unofficle way is download "Samfirm" and than download the firmware over this tool without an Account.
